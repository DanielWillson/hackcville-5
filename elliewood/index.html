The Elliewood Fellowship is a one-year program that gives top graduates a viable path to start ventures in Charlottesville and contribute to the local entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Charlottesville needs skilled graduates to work on big problems. Yet cultural and financial barriers discourage students from taking the non-traditional path and tackling important, meaningful problems to make a real community impact.

The Elliewood Fellowship enables this non-traditional path. It gives talented graduates a support structure to pursue an entrepreneurial career right here in Charlottesville.

About HackCville

HackCville develops the skills, networks, and entrepreneurial ability of talented U.Va. students. We accelerate our students’ ideas, projects, and startups through our experiential programs and tight-knit community.

In turn, we provide talent to 50+ companies in Charlottesville’s growing tech and startup scene and empower top students to start companies of their own. Our home is our clubhouses near the University of Virginia, but our broader network of 1,200+ spans the globe. More about us →
