Charlottesville needs skilled graduates to work on big problems.

Yet cultural and financial barriers discourage graduates from taking the non-traditional path they often really want: tackling important, meaningful problems and making a real community impact.

The Elliewood Fellowship enables that path, giving talented HackCville graduates a support structure to pursue an entrepreneurial career right here in Charlottesville.
