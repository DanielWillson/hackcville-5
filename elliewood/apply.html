Apply to the Elliewood Fellowship

Applications for Class of 2019 graduates will open in the Fall of 2018. You can express early interest in the program by submitting a quick form here.

The Fellowship is open exclusively to members of HackCville. However, if you are not currently involved in HackCville and are still interested, we still recommend you submit early interest above. We do recommend you get involved at HackCville via one of our programs or trips.

If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to Daniel Willson at daniel@hackcville.com.

About HackCville

HackCville develops the skills, networks, and entrepreneurial ability of talented U.Va. students. We accelerate our students’ ideas, projects, and startups through our experiential programs and tight-knit community.

In turn, we provide talent to 50+ companies in Charlottesville’s growing tech and startup scene and empower top students to start companies of their own. Our home is our clubhouses near the University of Virginia, but our broader network of 1,200+ spans the globe. More about us →
