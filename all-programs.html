HackCville's Programs

HackCville offers free 12-week programs in everything from entrepreneurship to photography to data science. We'll help you learn modern skills through fun, hands-on projects in a fast-paced, community driven environment.

Our programs are open to any UVa student (undergrad/grad) or Charlottesville resident 18+. Once you complete your first HackCville program, you're granted HackCville membership which comes with many of the perks detailed below.

Applications for Fall 2017 programs are open August 18 and close Monday August 28 at 11:59pm. Links to our applications can be found on specific program pages below. You may apply to up to two programs, but if you are selected for both you must pick only one to participate in.

Here are our programs, split into two categories.

Learn a Skill

Vector

Vector is our graphic design program. You learn design principles: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign; and develop a small portfolio of work.

Storyboard

Storyboard is our video production and storytelling program. You learn skills in interviewing, filming, and editing and publish your own work to HackCville's publication, The Pioneer.

The Node

The Node is our data science program. You learn the fundamentals of data science, how it is used to solve problems, and gain valuable technical experience.

Wireframe

Wireframe is our web design program. You learn the basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript and create your own personal space.

Exposure

Exposure is our photography program. You learn the basics of digital photography and editing and how you can become a freelance photographer.

Software Engineering

In our software engineering program you learn a curriculum that was invented by someone who actually knows a lot about software engineering. Who knew! Lit!

Digital Marketing

This is our digital marketing program. Its pretty cool. You will learn skills like SEO.

Start Something

Hustle

Hustle is our entrepreneurship program. You learn methods, mindsets, and skills essential for self starting. You'll utilize these skills through independent and community initiatives

Spark

Spark is our education, innovation, and entrepreneurship program. You learn and work on today's innovations in teacher training, ed tech, after school education, charter schools ect.

Ignite

Ignite is our social entrepreneurship program. You explore social issues, learn to tackle real-world problems, and apply knowledge through hands-on community projects.

Alpha

Alpha is our new program for people with existing projects or projects that continue after starting in Hustle, Spark, or Ignite. We provide personalized mentorship and resources to help you continue to grow your project.

Beta

Beta is our program for people with existing projects or projects that continue after starting in Hustle, Spark, or Ignite. We provide personalized mentorship and resources to help you continue to grow your project.

Launch

Get paid to learn coding, web design, marketing, or data science this summer.
Become a software engineer, web designer, marketer, or data scientist and experience an immersive summer internship at a top Charlottesville startup. Best of all, we'll pay you $1,000 to do it, all 12 weeks of the summer.
