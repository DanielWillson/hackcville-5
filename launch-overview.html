Get paid to learn this summer.

Choose from six immersive tracks spanning from Videography to Coding to Web Design. Experience an immersive summer internship at a top Charlottesville startup. Get paid $1300 to do it. Training begins Spring 2018.

Learn More Apply Now

Learn in-demand, critical skills.

You'll spend the first 4 weeks of the summer becoming expert in software engineering, web design, data science, digital marketing, graphic design, or videography. No previous experience is required. You'll learn these skills through fun, interactive projects with guidance from our expert instructors.

Build broad industry expertise.

Each week you'll explore a different industry through company tours, one-on-one mentorship, interactive workshops, and job shadowing. We'll focus on industries like healthcare, energy, and food. You'll add industry leaders to your network while gaining a deep understanding of our world's biggest challenges. You'll use this knowledge in the third and final part of the summer.

Build experience at a startup internship.

You'll take the skills you've learned from the first half of the program and put them to work at a local startup internship. We've partnered with top companies across Charlottesville to give you hands-on experience and real-world projects. You'll walk away with new skills and new experience to launch you toward whatever's next.

Our team of expert instructors has more than 100+ years of combined experience in tech and entrepreneurship.
